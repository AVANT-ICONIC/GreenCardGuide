# Disclaimer

This project provides **informational preparation guidance only**.

It is **not legal advice**, does **not** create an attorney-client relationship, and does **not** replace official instructions from the U.S. government, a consulate, a visa service provider, or a qualified immigration professional.

## What this project aims to do
- help applicants and sponsors prepare documents more clearly
- reduce avoidable confusion around interviews, intake, medical exams, and appointment logistics
- make public information easier to understand in English and Spanish

## What this project does not do
- guarantee eligibility, approval, or admission
- make legal judgments about complex immigration issues
- replace individualized legal advice
- guarantee that any checklist or page is complete for every case

## High-risk situations
Users should seek qualified legal help for issues such as:
- criminal history
- prior immigration violations
- waivers
- inadmissibility concerns
- custody, adoption, or identity-document complications
- unusual document availability issues

## Verify against official sources
Even when this repository is carefully maintained, official instructions control. Users should verify important requirements against the relevant official sources before acting.

## No warranty
The repository and its contents are provided as-is, without guarantees of completeness, timeliness, or fitness for any particular purpose.
